rComments

Free Reddit Chrome Extension for quick and hassle-free traversal of Reddit comments

Download Chrome Extension

So, what is this?

rComments is a Chrome extension that lets Redditors traverse through comments and their replies directly from the front pages of any subreddit. All you have to do is hover over the 'comments' link - a popup appears allowing you to read through the top comments and their replies, as well as upvote or downvote them if you'd like.

Alright not bad... how much is it?

Completely free.

What about privacy?

The extension is stateless - it does not store any information at all. All it needs is permission to read and change data on reddit.com sites, which is necessary in order fetch and render comments.
